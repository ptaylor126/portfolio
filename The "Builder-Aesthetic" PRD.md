The "Builder-Aesthetic" PRD
1. The Core Philosophy
The Hook: "I design apps. Then I build them."

The Mood: Premium, editorial, high-performance. Like a digital version of a high-end architecture magazine.

The Skill Flex: Instead of talking about process (which can get wordy and "junior"), we show it through Case Study Diversity. Each project card highlights a different "Superpower":

MealMatch AI: AI UX & Research.

Ballrs: Engineering & Shipping.

JetSweat/AllergyTracker: Metrics & Growth.

2. The Interaction Model (The "Non-Template" Secret)
The "Magnetic" Header: A header that feels anchored but reacts to the scroll depth.

The "Canvas Slide": Instead of a simple scroll, the background color of the entire site morphs smoothly based on which project is in the viewport (e.g., #F6F1EB for the hero, shifting to a slightly deeper tone for MealMatch).

The "Micro-Glint": Interactive hover states on project cards that use a "flashlight" effect (CSS radial-gradients) to reveal a "Behind the Scenes" wireframe layer underneath the high-fidelity mock.
